Welcome to Primavera

Primavera is an app which aims to support women going through menopause or who want to prepare themselves before this period of their lives.

An on-boarding questionnaire will help us understand your pain points and get a better insight of your health condition. Thus, we will be able to position you at the menopause journey map and then provide you with relevant information and techniques to guide you through your current stage in life.
